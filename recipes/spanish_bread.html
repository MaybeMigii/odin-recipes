Quick and Easy Recipe for Spanish Bread

Description

Spanish bread, despite it's name, is actually a pretty common Filipino dessert. You'll find it in popular bakeries such as Golden coin or Valerios and is a very sweet and delicious snack. Although it looks like a hard dessert to make, this dish is actually really simple and can be easily made with ingredients found at home!

Whether you're trying to surprise your Filipino friends or family, or just want a quick and easy snack to make this recipe will definitely satisfy your needs.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of regular white bread
  • Spoonful of sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Take out all of your ingredients
  2. Slather butter all over the slice of bread
  3. Spread your spoonful of sugar all over the bread as evenly as possible
  4. Fold the bread in half so that the sugar would be inside the fold
  5. Microwave for about 20 seconds
  6. Take out the spanish bread, serve on a plate, and enjoy!
    • These are very sweet, so try not to eat too much if you're on a diet.
