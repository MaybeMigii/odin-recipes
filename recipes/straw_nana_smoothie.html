Summer vibe Strawnana Smoothie

Description

This type of smoothie is best for the summer times, but drink it whenever you feel like it! Strawnana is something I've been drinking as a kid and it's always such a tasty treat.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Bananas
  • Vanilla Ice Cream
  • Apple Juice
  • Ice

Tools

  • Blender

Steps

  1. Fill the blender with about 9 oz. of apple juice
  2. Throw 2 scoops of vanilla ice cream into blender
  3. Throw two scoop of strawberries into blender
  4. Throw one scoop of bananas into blender
  5. Throw one scoop of ice into blender
  6. Blend
  7. Pour into cup and enjoy!
