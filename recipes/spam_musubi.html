Deep Fried Spam Musubi Recipe

Description

Some of you may have heard of or have tried a spam musubi, but something most people haven't tried is a deep fried spam musubi. This is a type of musubi that you'll most likely only find in Hawaii, and will definitely give you high cholestorol

Each bite of this musubi will definitely have you craving more,and will make you wonder why you've never had it before. However, this is definitely something you should only eat every so often due to how oily it can be.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Spam
  • White Rice
  • Seaweed Wrap
  • Panko Bread Crumbs
  • 1 Dozen Eggs
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Vegetable Oil

Tools

  • Musubi Molder

Steps

  1. Cook a pot of white rice
  2. Cook the can of spam
    • Cut it into equally thick slices to get enough taste out of it
  3. Crack open the eggs and whisk the yolk and egg whites
  4. Place the seaweed on a flat surface
  5. Place the musubi molder on top of it
  6. Place a moderate amount of rice on top of the seaweed in the mold
  7. Place the sliced spam on top of the rice
  8. Place a moderate amount of rice on top of the spam
  9. Flatten it using the musubi molder
  10. Wrap the rice and spam with seaweed
  11. Coat the musubi in the all purpose flour
  12. Coat the musubi in the egg mix
  13. Coat the musubi in the panko bread crumbs
  14. Deep fry the musubi in the vegetable oil
  15. Serve on a plate and enjoy!
